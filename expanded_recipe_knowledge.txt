Recipe: Banana Oats Smoothie
Ingredients: banana, oats, milk, honey (optional)

Steps:
1. Peel and slice the banana.
2. Add banana, oats, and milk into a blender.
3. Add honey if sweetness is desired.
4. Blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy.
5. Pour into a glass and serve chilled.

Tips:
- Use frozen banana for a thicker smoothie.
- Soak oats for 10 minutes for a smoother texture.


Recipe: Tomato Rice
Ingredients: rice, tomato, cumin, salt, oil, garlic, onion

Steps:
1. Cook rice and set it aside.
2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin and chopped garlic.
3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
5. Mix in cooked rice and stir well.
6. Add salt to taste and cook for 2 more minutes.

Tips:
- Garnish with coriander for added freshness.


Recipe: Spicy Potato Pea Curry
Ingredients: potato, green peas, onion, tomato, turmeric, chili powder, oil, salt

Steps:
1. Boil and cube the potatoes.
2. Heat oil, add chopped onions, sauté until translucent.
3. Add tomatoes, cook until mushy.
4. Mix in turmeric and chili powder.
5. Add peas and potatoes, stir and cook for 5–7 minutes.
6. Add water for desired consistency and simmer.

Tips:
- Serve with rice or flatbread.
- Add garam masala for a richer flavor.


Recipe: Chickpea Salad
Ingredients: boiled chickpeas, onion, tomato, lemon juice, salt, pepper

Steps:
1. Combine chickpeas, diced onions, and tomatoes in a bowl.
2. Squeeze lemon juice over the mix.
3. Add salt and pepper to taste.
4. Toss everything well and serve chilled.

Tips:
- Add chopped coriander or mint for extra freshness.


Recipe: Grilled Cheese Tomato Sandwich
Ingredients: bread, cheese slices, tomato, butter

Steps:
1. Butter two slices of bread.
2. Place cheese and tomato slices between them.
3. Heat a pan and grill sandwich until golden on both sides.
4. Slice and serve warm.

Tips:
- Add herbs like oregano for more flavor.
